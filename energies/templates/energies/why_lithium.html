Why Choose Lithium-ion Batteries?

Lithium-ion batteries are the future of clean, efficient, and long-lasting energy. At Samyati Energies, we use cutting-edge lithium technology to power e-rickshaws and other electric vehicles in Nepal and beyond.

🔋 Longer Life Cycle

Up to 5x longer lifespan than traditional lead-acid batteries — reducing replacement costs.

⚡ Fast Charging

Spend less time charging and more time on the road with rapid charging technology.

🏋️ Lightweight & Compact

Designed for mobility — our batteries reduce vehicle weight while maximizing performance.

🌱 Eco-Friendly

No harmful emissions or toxic leaks — lithium-ion batteries are safer for the environment.

🔧 Low Maintenance

No topping up or frequent servicing required. Just install and go!

🚀 High Energy Density

More power in a smaller package — giving your EVs better range and reliability.
